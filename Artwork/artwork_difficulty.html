Difficulty Level Considerations

In order to cater to all age groups and varying visual proficiency abilities of our players, game plays of the respective difficulty levels are slightly different to accommodate all of our players. The decision was to split the game into 4 different difficulty levels - Beginner I , Beginner II, Novice, Expert.

Within each level, the difficulty of each round increases slightly, so as to build a continuously increasing challenge to our players.

As such, players will be able to choose a difficulty level of your own comfort and progressively build up your visual proficiency skills.

Intermediate Sketches

The team came up with a few questions to determine what type of questionnaire should be shown to the players at varying difficulty levels.

Type 1

Following the card shown, players to recreate the exact same pattern.

Players can start with the card being shown to them all the time as they recreate the pattern.

Progressively, as players get more used to playing, it can be modified to a memory game where the card will only be shown for a time limit. Players are then to recreate the pattern based on their memory.
